Meet Our Wonderful Residents

From the mighty lions to the playful penguins, discover the incredible diversity of wildlife at our zoo.

Bengal Tiger

The majestic Bengal Tiger is a symbol of power and the largest of all Asian big cats.

African Elephant

The world's largest land animal, known for its incredible intelligence and strong family bonds.

Giraffe

With their towering legs and long necks, giraffes are the world's tallest mammals.

Red Panda

A fluffy, tree-dwelling mammal native to the eastern Himalayas and southwestern China.

Lion

Known as the "king of the jungle," this social cat lives in groups called prides.

Zebra

Every zebra has a unique pattern of black and white stripes, much like a human fingerprint.

Penguin

These flightless birds are expert swimmers, perfectly adapted for life in the sea.

Peacock

Famous for the male's iridescent tail feathers, which he displays to attract a mate.

Gorilla

Gentle giants that are one of our closest living relatives, sharing about 98% of our DNA.

Kangaroo

A marsupial from Australia, known for its powerful hind legs used for leaping great distances.

Snow Leopard

An elusive and beautiful big cat native to the mountain ranges of Central and South Asia.

Komodo Dragon

The world's largest living lizard, found in the Indonesian islands. A powerful and fearsome predator.
